Gut Check: What is happening?

This demonstrates a Convolutional Layer, the standard for image processing.

The Math (Simplified)

The "Filter" (middle) slides over your image like a magnifying glass. It performs a dot product (multiplication + sum) for every 3x3 chunk of your image.

Score = (Pixel * Weight) + (Pixel * Weight) ...

Why does it light up?

Notice the filter has positive numbers (1) in the middle column and negative numbers (-1) on the sides.

  • If you draw a vertical line: The white pixels line up with the positive weights. 1x1 = 1. Big score! The neuron fires (Yellow).
  • If you draw a horizontal line: The white pixels hit both positive AND negative weights. They cancel each other out (1 + -1 = 0). The neuron stays dark.

Intuition: A hidden layer is just a set of filters, each designed to "shout" when it sees a specific pattern and "stay silent" when it sees noise.
